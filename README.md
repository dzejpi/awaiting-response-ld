# Awaiting Response
My videogame for Ludum Dare #59. Theme: "Signal."

You are not sure how you got here. But it feels like something is trying to communicate with you. It feels like stronger you are to the signal, the closer you are to something important. 
Follow it. 
Trust it.

## Game tips

* **Don't** overthink it.
* **Let** the signal guide you.
* ~~**It** becomes stronger the more you engage with it.~~
* **Find** what responds to you and act on it.
* **You** won't progress by standing still.

## Controls

* WSAD: Move like in all 3D first person games.
* E: Respond to signal.
* Escape: Toggle pause menu. If you're playing HTML5 version, press Esc twice and click on the Continue button with your mouse to focus it back properly; this is the limitation of the web platform, unfortunately.
* Shift (hold): Sprint.
* Space: Jump.
